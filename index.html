statement

Statement

The inner message that speaks from a work of art is more important to me than the medium and technique chosen. However, in my new body of work it is the materiality that creates the message and excites and inspires. I play, explore and respond. I extract what is there and take the material to new places, stretching them and myself.

This often leads to surprising and exciting results.

The process in itself is significant. While the playfulness and experience of pulling, breaking, fragmenting, photographing and printing fuels my imagination, the making itself is thought through and realised with skill and passion.

All materials are sourced from distinctly different places. Contextualizing or juxtaposing them allow to disclose their hidden narrative.
